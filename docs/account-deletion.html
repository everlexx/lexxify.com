Account Deletion

Deleting your data at Lexxify is easy. The following is a non-exhaustive list of data that will be deleted in the process:

  • • Your profile data (name, username, biography, etc.)
  • • Your profile photo
  • • Your posts and any related files
  • • Your post likes
  • • Your post comments and any related files

There's are 2 ways you can have your account deleted:

  • 1. By using the mobile app (recommended)
  • 2. By sending us an email (takes longer to be processed)

Follow the steps below to delete your account using the recommended, in-app method:

  • 1. Open Lexxify Hub mobile app on your smartphone
  • 2. Sign in if you're not already signed in
  • 3. Towards the bottom right of the screen, click the '⚙️' icon to open settings
  • 4. Select 'Account deletion' from the list
  • 5. Click 'Delete account' and confirm your action
