This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and process information from and about users of Lexxify Hub. By using Lexxify Hub, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

As with other social media platforms, during the course of your usage of our apps, products, or other services, our service may receive information from and about you, including information that you provide, information from other sources, and other automatically inferred information.

Information/Data You Provide

When you create an account, upload content, contact us directly, or otherwise use the Platform, you may provide some or all of the following information: account and profile information, such as name, age, username, password, language, email, phone number, social media account information, and profile image. After registering for a Lexxify account, you may access, review, and/or edit/update certain personal information that you have provided to Lexxify by logging into your account going to the Settings.

If you select to sign-up or log-in to Lexxify Hub using a third-party service such as Facebook or Google, or otherwise link your Lexxify account to a third-party service, this process will prompt our service to access your public profile information on that platform, such as email and friends list.

By letting Lexxify access your device services, the service automatically collects certain information from you when you use Lexxify Hub, including internet or other network activity information such as your IP address, geolocation-related data, unique device identifiers, browsing and search history (including content you have viewed in Lexxify Hub), and Cookies. Cookies help Lexxify collect information and analyze usage of our platform, and help us enhance the user experience by giving us data on how best to efficiently format the user experience. To disable certain Cookies, please refer to your browser settings and options. Note that having certain Cookies disabled may affect your ability to access the full range of Lexxify Hub functionality and services.

Our service will collect and store any user-generated content that you post or upload to Lexxify Hub, such as images, audio, videos, weblinks, hashtags, and comments. This also includes any messages you may send to other users in the chat functionality. The service will also collect and store the associated metadata, such as when, where, and by whom the content was created. If you apply an edit/filter/effect to an image or audio or other data if applicable the service may collect and store a version before the respective edits/filter/effect are applied. We may collect information about the videos, images, and audio that are a part of your user content, such as identifying the objects and scenery that appear, the existence and location within an image of physical features and attributes, the nature of the audio, and the text of the words in audio in your post. We may collect this information to enable special video effects, for content moderation, to improve user experience, to provide recommendations for other users, for demographic analysis, for effective content and ad recommendations, and for other non-personally-identifying operations.

When you upload or create content, you automatically upload certain metadata that is connected to that content. Metadata refers to other data related to the content and provides information about your content that will not always be viewable to users. The metadata can describe how, when, where, and by whom the content was created, collected, or modified, or how long the audio is or how large the data is.

In order to provide services, we also access the information, including text, images, and videos, found in your device's clipboard, when you provide it to us by copying or pasting content in Lexxify Hub. For example, if you choose to paste content from the clipboard into Lexxify Hub, we access this information stored in your clipboard so that we can carry out your action on our system.

Our service stores messages, which include information you provide when you compose, send, or receive messages through the Platform's messaging functionalities. This includes messages you send through our chat functionality. That information includes the content of the message and information about the message, such as when it was sent, received, or read, and message participants. Messages you choose to send to other users of Lexxify Hub will be accessible by those users and that we are not responsible for the manner in which those users use or share the messages.

Lexxify Hub accesses your phone and social network contacts, with your permission. If you choose to find other users through your phone contacts, we will access and collect information such as names, phone numbers, and email addresses, and match that information against existing users of the Platform. If you choose to find other users through your social network contacts, we will collect your profile information that you have made accessible as well as names and profiles of your social network contacts.

Lexxify also accesses Information to verify an account such as email address or age. Lexxify also accesses information in correspondence you send to us, including when you contact us for support, as well as any information you share through surveys or your participation in challenges, research, promotions, marketing campaigns

Automatically Collected Information/Data

We collect information about how you use Lexxify Hub, including your interactions with other users, your search queries, and the content you view.

As with other social media platforms, we collect information about the devices you use to access Lexxify Hub, including the type of device, operating system, IP address, time zone, mobile carrier, and browser type, screen resolution, battery level, audio settings, and connected audio devices. If you log-in to Lexxify from multiple devices, we may need to use information such as device ID and user ID to keep track of your activity across devices.

As with other social media platforms, we collect information about your approximate location, including location information based on your SIM card and/or IP address in order to serve you better. In addition, we collect location information (such as tourist attractions, shops, or other points of interest) if you choose to provide such location information.
